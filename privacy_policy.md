# Privacy Policy for Earnegg Telegram Mini App

**Last Updated:** March 27, 2026

At **Earnegg**, we are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a transparent experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you interact with the Earnegg Telegram Mini App ("the App").

---

### 1. Information We Collect

*   **Telegram User Data**: We collect your Telegram User ID, Username, and First Name (as provided by the Telegram Web App SDK) to uniquely identify you and store your progress.
*   **Usage Data**: We track your in-app actions, such as task completions, mini-game scores, egg collections, and coin balance, to facilitate the "Play-to-Earn" mechanics.
*   **Device Info**: Our third-party ad partners may collect non-identifiable device information (e.g., operating system, browser type, and IP address) to serve relevant advertisements.

### 2. How We Use Informatiom

*   **To Provide the Service**: To authenticate your account, balance rewards, and allow you to view your progress.
*   **Fraud Prevention**: To ensure a fair environment and prevent the use of multiple accounts or automated bots.
*   **Monetization**: To show advertisements that allow us to keep the service free and provide rewards to users.

### 3. Third-Party Services

We integrate with third-party providers who may collect or process your data:
*   **Telegram Messenger**: The platform that hosts the App.
*   **Supabase (Database)**: For secure cloud storage and data handling.
*   **AdsGram (Rewarded Ads)**: For serving task-based rewarded advertisements.
*   **Adsterra & Monetag (Native Banners)**: For displaying visual advertisements.
*   **Google Fonts**: For rendering custom typography.

*Note: These third parties have their own privacy policies. We do not share your private account data with them, but they may collect anonymized IP/Device data to serve ads.*

### 4. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active on Earnegg. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact our support team.

### 5. Security

We take appropriate technical measures to protect your data stored on Supabase using Row-Level Security (RLS) and secure authentication practices.

### 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
*   Access your stored coin balance and task history.
*   Request the deletion of your account/data at any time.
*   Opt-out of optional analytical tracking if implemented in the future.

### 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests regarding your data, please reach out via our official Telegram support:
*   **Support Bot/Username**: @EarneggSupport (Example)
