# 🚀 Future Features Roadmap: Earnegg

This document outlines potential features and improvements for the Earnegg Telegram Mini App based on current trends in the "Tap-to-Earn" ecosystem and user engagement best practices.

---

## 🎮 1. Gaming & Engagement
*   **Mini-Game Hub**: Integrate simple HTML5 games (like Flappy Egg, Egg Match-3) where players can spend a small amount of energy to win big coin rewards.
*   **Player Levels & Ranks**: Introduce a level system (e.g., Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum). Higher levels increase energy capacity and unlock exclusive tasks.
*   **Daily Combo/Secret Code**: A daily puzzle where users must find or guess a combination to earn a massive daily bonus (highly effective for community building on Telegram).
*   **Spin the Wheel**: A daily luck-based mechanic for coins, energy refills, or temporary boosts.
*   **Airdrop Eligibility Meter**: A roadmap or progress bar showing users how close they are to being eligible for a potential future token airdrop.

## 👥 2. Social & Viral Growth
*   **Squads (Clans)**: Allow users to join or create "Squads." Squads compete on a separate leaderboard, and top squads get weekly rewards distributed among members.
*   **Gifting System**: Let users send a portion of their coins to friends or "red envelopes" (AngPao) in group chats.
*   **Group Chat Integration**: A bot that announces when a squad member reaches a new level or makes a large withdrawal, encouraging others to play.
*   **Milestone Rewards**: Extra bonuses for reaching referral milestones (e.g., "Refer 10 friends and get a permanent +5% tap bonus").

## 💰 3. Economy & Monetization
*   **Native TON Wallet Connect**: Integrate `@tonconnect/sdk` to allow users to link their TON wallets directly for faster/automated crypto withdrawals.
*   **Staking Mechanic**: Let users "lock" their coins for a period (e.g., 7 days) to earn interest (e.g., 10% more coins).
*   **NFT Boosters**: Unique "Egg Skins" or "Tap Tools" that are NFTs. Holding them provides permanent boosts to earning speed or referral commissions.
*   **Premium Membership (Telegram Star-based)**: Use Telegram Stars to offer a "Pro" version with no interstitial ads, faster energy recharge, and double daily rewards.

## 🎨 4. UI/UX & Retention
*   **Custom Themes/Skins**: Allow users to customize the look of their "Egg" and the background using coins or premium currency.
*   **Soundscapes & SFX**: Add high-quality sound effects for tapping, winning rewards, and navigating (with a toggle to mute).
*   **Localization (Multi-language Support)**: Support for Hindi, Russian, Spanish, and other major Telegram user bases to expand the reach.
*   **Push Notifications (Bot-based)**: Use the Telegram Bot to send reminders when energy is full or the Tap Bot has reached its 12h limit.

## 🛠️ 5. Technical & Security
*   **Anti-Cheat System**: Implement server-side validation for tap frequency and pattern detection to prevent auto-clicker abuse.
*   **Advanced Analytics Dashbord**: For admins to track DAU (Daily Active Users), Retention Rates, and Withdrawal trends more effectively.
*   **Dynamic Task System**: A web-based admin panel where you can add new tasks (Twitter follows, YouTube watches) without updating the app code.
*   **Public API/Webhooks**: For third-party partners to verify if a user has completed a certain level in Earnegg.

---

> [!TIP]
> **Priority Suggestion**: Focus on **Squads** and **Daily Combos** first. These features create the "social proof" and "daily routine" needed to break into the top tier of Telegram Mini Apps.
